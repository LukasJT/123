Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about what 123Videos is and how the catalog works.

Is 123Videos a streaming site?

No. 123Videos is an informational movie and TV catalog. It does not host, stream, or link to full video files.

What does a title page include?

Title pages include catalog details such as synopsis, year, genre, rating, poster, duration, and related recommendations.

Why are there ads?

Ads and sponsored links help support the catalog. Advertising is labeled and third-party advertisers are responsible for their own policies.

How do I report a problem?

Use the contact page for corrections or the DMCA page for copyright concerns.
